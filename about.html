About Us

Since its inception in 2015, Toshani is determined to reshape the world of digital payments, first nationally and then globally. We are a technology-driven company on its to building India’s largest network of last-mile retail outlets to deliver our premium services to the unserved and the underserved rural, semi-urban and urban population of the country. Our well-crafted Assisted Digital Convenience stores are called as ‘Nextgen Toshani Kendras’. Each store is equipped as ‘One Stop Shop’ to avail multiple products and services.

Toshani is a premier payment solutions provider empowering banks and merchant aggregators with a comprehensive array of technology-led cash and digital payment solutions. Our solutions are crafted towards customer satisfaction and have redefined the ease of payments and transactions. Our collaborative efforts with our expert team have resulted in breakthrough distribution network. We started as a young, dynamic, and vivacious company and now are growing more innovative and agile day after day year after year while keeping our initial traits intact.
